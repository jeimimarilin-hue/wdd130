About Us

Our mission is to provide safe and exhilarating rafting experiences. We are a team of passionate guides dedicated to the river and your adventure.

History

Since our founding in 2005, we have been a family-owned business. We started with just one raft and a small dream in the heart of the mountains.

Today, we serve thousands of adventurers every year while maintaining our commitment to environmental sustainability and top-tier safety standards.
